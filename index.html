I am a Software Engineer working in Vision Research Group at Samsung Research, Bangalore. My work primarily revovles around Computer Vision and Machine Learning and currently, I am working on AI-based in-loop filters to improve Video Compression efficiency. My research interests are in building generalizable systems which can understand the visual world as well as humans do.

I graduated with a B.E. in Computer Science from RV College of Engineering, India in 2020. For my thesis, I worked on Depth Completion & Upscaling of images from time-of-flight sensor in collaboration with Samsung. After college, I interned at a deep-tech startup, Curl Analytics where I worked on Document Understanding from commodity trading companies. In my 3rd year summer, I interned at Samsung to work on Video Analysis pipelines and in my 2nd year summer, at ZoomTail technologies to work on building a Content Management System.

At college outside of the classroom, I worked on a nano-satellite project at Team Antariksh, curated content for the TEDx team and participated in several Hackathons. I was also an active member in a community Toastmasters Club.

If you can't find me building cool tech, you will find me either trying to nutmeg someone on a football field or trekking serene mountains or deeply engrossed in a non-fiction book.
